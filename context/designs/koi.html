Our Mission
Why KOI exists

Shopping from global brands in Nigeria is hard — foreign currency, no local delivery, unreliable agents. KOI removes all that friction: you browse real global products in one polished place, pay the full price in naira, and we handle the rest.

One Naira Price
You never leave KOI to pay anyone else. One naira price, one checkout, one place to track your order — from Lagos to London and back.
Our Story
Built for the Nigerian shopper

We started KOI after watching friends and family pay agents double the sticker price — or get scammed outright — just to own a pair of sneakers or a skincare routine sold everywhere else in the world. KOI is the fix: real brands, one fair naira price, delivered properly.

Trust & Safety
🛍️
How does KOI work?
⌄
You browse global brands on KOI, add items to your cart, and pay the full naira price at checkout. KOI then buys the item from the brand, ships it internationally, and delivers it to your address in Nigeria — you never pay anyone else.
💳
Do I need a dollar card or foreign currency?
⌄
No. You pay entirely in naira via Paystack. KOI handles the currency conversion and international purchase on your behalf.
🧮
How is the naira price calculated?
⌄
The price shown includes the product's cost, currency conversion, international shipping, and KOI's delivery fee — all in one number, with nothing added later.
🏷️
Which brands can I shop on KOI?
⌄
500+ global fashion, beauty, and tech brands including Nike, Zara, Sephora, Gucci, Apple, Adidas and Fenty Beauty — with more added regularly.
📏
What if the size or colour I want is sold out?
⌄
If an item goes out of stock at the brand after you order, we'll contact you immediately to offer an alternative size, colour, or a full refund.
🚚
How long does delivery take?
⌄
Most orders arrive in 7–14 days from payment, depending on the brand and destination. Delays can occur due to customs or vendor availability.
📦
How do I track my order?
⌄
Use the Track Order page with your order reference and checkout email to see live status updates from purchase through delivery.
⚠️
What if my order arrives damaged or doesn't arrive?
⌄
Contact us with your order reference and photos (if damaged) and we'll investigate with our logistics partner and make it right.
🔒
Is my payment secure?
⌄
Yes. All payments are processed by Paystack — KOI never sees or stores your card details.
↩️
What's your return policy?
⌄
Items that arrive damaged, defective, or different from what you ordered are eligible for a refund or replacement. See the Returns page for full details.
💬
How do I get help with an order?
⌄
Reach us on WhatsApp or email with your order reference — our team responds within a few hours.

Returns & refunds
Because KOI sources every item from the brand on your behalf, our full return policy is being finalised alongside our logistics partners.
🚧 Draft policy — the guidelines below reflect our general approach while the full policy is confirmed with our shipping partners.
✅ Generally eligible
  • Item arrives damaged or defective
  • Wrong item, size, or colour delivered
  • Item is significantly different from its listing
🚫 Not eligible
  • Change of mind after the item has shipped from the brand
  • Items worn, used, or with tags removed
  • Customs and import fees, once processed

1Information we collect

When you place an order, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and delivery address so we can fulfil and deliver your order. When you pay, Paystack processes your payment details directly — KOI never sees or stores your card number.

2How we use your information

We use your information to process and deliver your order, communicate with you about its status, and respond to support requests. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3How your information is stored

Order and account data is stored with our database provider, InsForge. Payments are processed by Paystack under their own security standards.

4Your choices

You can contact us at any time to ask what information we hold about you or to request it be corrected or deleted, subject to what we're required to keep for order and payment records.
